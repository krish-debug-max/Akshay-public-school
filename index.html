About Our School

Where Every Child
Discovers Their Potential

Akshay Public School, located in the heart of Lakhpat Colony, Mithapur, New Delhi, is a recognized English medium institution committed to providing quality education that blends academic rigour with cultural enrichment.

Our approach to learning goes beyond textbooks. We believe in nurturing well-rounded individuals who are not only academically competent but also grounded in values, discipline, and a sense of community.
